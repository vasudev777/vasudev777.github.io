About me

A bit about
who I am

I'm Vasudev Parmar, a B.Tech Computer Science graduate from Parul University, Vadodara (CGPA 8.24). I don't just write code — I build things that work in the real world. Three of my projects are already live, including Shram, a labour hiring marketplace backed by published research paper. That's not a college assignment — that's a product.

I've cleared GATE in both Computer Science (2024 & 2026) and Data Science & AI (2026) — not because I had to, but because I push until the job is done. I'm deeply interested in the technologies shaping the next decade: AI & ML, Agentic AI systems, Large Language Models, RAG pipelines, Cloud infrastructure on AWS, and full-stack development with modern frameworks. I believe the most meaningful work happens at the intersection of technology and real human problems — and that's exactly where I want to be. I'm here for the long game.
